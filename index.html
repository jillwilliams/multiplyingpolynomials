Multiplying Complex Numbers

To multiply complex numbers, enter the equation into the boxes below.
The types of problems this program will solve are:

  1.  (5 + 3i)(3 + 2i)
  2.  (7 - 6i)(4 + 8i)

  • Once you have entered in your values, click the submit button for the solution.
  • Make sure to enter your numbers into the boxes as it appears written above, in order from left to right.
  • The term with the "i" should be entered second for each binomial, and the constant should be entered first for each binomial.
  • Enter your numbers below.

( + i ) ( + i )
